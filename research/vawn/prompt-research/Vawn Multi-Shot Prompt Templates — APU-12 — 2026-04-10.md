## Template 1: Performance Core (Rap Delivery)

### 16:9 Cinematic Version
```
Subject: @VawnRef stands in an abandoned warehouse, wearing (@VawnOutfit1), 
delivering bars with controlled intensity and precise hand gestures.

Action: He maintains steady eye contact with camera while rapping, 
subtle head movement matching the rhythm, hands emphasizing key phrases.

Environment: Industrial warehouse space with exposed brick walls, 
hanging tungsten work lights, urban decay details in soft focus background.

Camera: Slow dolly push-in from medium shot to close-medium, 
camera height at eye level, steady gimbal movement.

Style: Warm tungsten key light from camera left, cool blue rim light 
from behind, cinematic grain, 35mm film emulation, shallow depth of field.

Constraints: Smooth camera motion, preserve composition and colors, 
no jitter, maintain sharp focus on subject.
```

### 9:16 Social Vertical Version
```
Subject: @VawnRef in tight medium shot, wearing (@VawnOutfit2), 
delivering verse directly to camera with authentic energy.

Action: Natural hand gestures matching lyrical flow, 
subtle shoulder movement, maintaining connection with lens.

Environment: Urban rooftop at golden hour, Atlanta skyline blurred 
in background, warm ambient lighting.

Camera: Static framing, locked off at shoulder height, 
no camera movement to maximize impact.

Style: Golden hour natural light as key, soft fill from reflector, 
warm color grading, high contrast, modern mobile aesthetic.

Constraints: Vertical composition, text-safe areas preserved, 
no motion blur, optimized for mobile viewing.
```

## Template 2: Urban Atmosphere (Environment Building)

### Establishing Shot
```
Subject: Wide view of Atlanta's urban landscape transitioning 
from day to night, city lights beginning to illuminate.

Action: Slow traffic flow on highways, distant figures moving 
through the streets, gradual transition of natural to artificial light.

Environment: Downtown Atlanta financial district with residential 
neighborhoods visible, realistic urban density.

Camera: Slow aerial drone movement, ascending and panning left to right, 
maintaining steady altitude throughout.

Style: Golden hour fading to blue hour, warm streetlight highlights 
against cool shadows, realistic color temperature shift.

Constraints: Smooth drone motion, no sudden altitude changes, 
preserve atmospheric depth, maintain exposure balance.
```

### Street Level Mood
```
Subject: Urban street corner at night, neon signs reflecting 
on wet asphalt, authentic Atlanta neighborhood vibe.

Action: Slow-moving traffic, pedestrians crossing in background, 
natural urban rhythm without forced activity.

Environment: Residential-commercial mix, local businesses, 
realistic lighting from storefronts and street lamps.

Camera: Low angle tracking shot along sidewalk level, 
following the natural flow of the street.

Style: Neon color palette with warm/cool contrast, 
cinematic night photography, realistic grain structure.

Constraints: Maintain streetlight realism, no oversaturated colors, 
preserve natural urban atmosphere.
```

## Template 3: Narrative Movement (Story Progression)

### Journey/Walking Sequence
```
Subject: @VawnRef walks purposefully through urban Atlanta streets, 
wearing (@VawnOutfit1), lost in thought but determined.

Action: Natural walking pace, hands in pockets, occasional 
glances at surroundings, authentic pedestrian movement.

Environment: Mixed residential-commercial Atlanta neighborhood, 
realistic storefronts, parked cars, everyday urban details.

Camera: Tracking shot from the side at waist height, 
maintaining consistent distance and framing.

Style: Natural daylight with soft overcast, realistic color grading, 
documentary-style authenticity, 35mm film grain.

Constraints: Match real walking speed, smooth tracking motion, 
preserve environmental authenticity, no artificial drama.
```

### Reflective Interior Moment
```
Subject: @VawnRef sits by large window in downtown apartment, 
wearing (@VawnOutfit3), contemplating the city view below.

Action: Subtle movements — adjusting position, looking from 
window to middle distance, natural contemplative gestures.

Environment: Modern apartment interior with floor-to-ceiling windows, 
Atlanta skyline visible, minimalist urban living space.

Camera: Slow push-in from wide to medium, camera positioned 
opposite the window to capture both subject and view.

Style: Natural window light as key, soft interior fill lighting, 
realistic exposure balance between interior and exterior.

Constraints: Preserve window exposure detail, smooth dolly movement, 
maintain mood without overdramatizing.
```

## Template 5: Lyric Focus (Close-Up Moments)

### Emotional Delivery
```
Subject: @VawnRef in tight close-up, wearing (@VawnOutfit1), 
delivering emotionally resonant bars with subtle intensity.

Action: Micro-expressions matching lyrical content, 
controlled breathing, eyes maintaining story connection.

Environment: Out-of-focus urban background with warm bokeh, 
suggestion of city lights or streetlamps.

Camera: Static close-up framing, locked off for maximum 
emotional impact and lyrical clarity.

Style: Single-source key light creating dramatic shadows, 
warm color temperature, high contrast for emotion.

Constraints: Perfect focus on eyes, no distracting movement, 
preserve emotional authenticity over technical perfection.
```

### Truth-Telling Moment
```
Subject: @VawnRef speaking directly to camera with quiet confidence, 
wearing (@VawnOutfit2), delivering personal truth.

Action: Minimal movement, occasional hand gesture for emphasis, 
authentic pause timing, real conversation feel.

Environment: Simple background — could be apartment, studio, 
or neutral urban space, nothing competing for attention.

Camera: Medium close-up, eye-level framing, completely stable 
to emphasize the directness of communication.

Style: Soft natural lighting, realistic skin tones, 
minimal color grading to preserve authenticity.

Constraints: No camera movement, focus on message delivery, 
preserve intimate conversation feeling.
```

## Template 6: Transition Bridges (Scene Connectors)

### Time Shift Transition
```
Subject: Urban environment showing passage of time — 
same location at different times of day.

Action: Time-lapse effect showing traffic patterns, 
lighting changes, natural rhythm of city life.

Environment: Key Atlanta location that appears in other sequences, 
maintaining visual continuity across the music video.

Camera: Fixed position for clean time transition, 
no movement to preserve location reference.

Style: Smooth color temperature shift from warm to cool, 
realistic time passage, cinematic transition feel.

Constraints: Maintain location continuity, smooth temporal progression, 
avoid artificial acceleration artifacts.
```

### Movement Transition
```
Subject: @VawnRef exits frame in one location, creating 
natural transition point between major sequences.

Action: Natural walking movement that leads viewer's eye 
toward frame edge, smooth exit timing.

Environment: Current sequence location transitioning to next, 
maintaining visual flow between scenes.

Camera: Static framing allowing subject to move through 
and exit, creating natural cut point.

Style: Match lighting and color grading of surrounding sequences, 
seamless visual integration.

Constraints: Clean exit timing, preserve visual continuity, 
avoid jarring transitions between sequences.
```

## Platform-Specific Adaptations

### YouTube (16:9 Primary)
- Use cinematic templates for main content
- Focus on narrative progression and visual storytelling
- Optimize for desktop viewing with detailed backgrounds

### TikTok/Instagram Reels (9:16)
- Prioritize social vertical templates
- Maximize impact in first 3 seconds
- Design for mobile viewing without sound

### Instagram Feed (1:1)
- Crop 16:9 templates to square format
- Focus on central composition elements
- Optimize for thumbnail clarity

## Technical Specifications

### Seedance 2.0 Settings
- **Duration**: 15 seconds maximum per generation
- **Aspect Ratio**: Set per platform (16:9, 9:16, 1:1)
- **Resolution**: Up to 2K (1920x1080 for 16:9)
- **Audio**: Upload mastered track from Vawn pipeline (Freq/Slate output)
- **Reference Images**: Maximum 9 images per generation (@VawnRef, @VawnOutfit1-3)

### Quality Control Checklist
- [ ] Character consistency maintained across shots
- [ ] Color grading matches Vawn's established aesthetic
- [ ] Camera movements are smooth and purposeful
- [ ] Lighting supports mood and visibility
- [ ] Composition optimized for target platform
- [ ] Audio sync verified (mute AI audio, use pipeline audio)

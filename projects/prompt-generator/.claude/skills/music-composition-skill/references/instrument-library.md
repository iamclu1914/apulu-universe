# Instrument Library Reference

## ⚠️ CRITICAL RULES

### Instruments Per Track
| Range | Result |
|-------|--------|
| **10-14 instruments** | ✅ Sweet spot - Suno generates consistently |
| Below 8 | ❌ Suno fills in defaults randomly |
| Above 15 | ❌ Suno blends/ignores, gets unpredictable |

### Composition Formula (Per Track)
Pick from each category:
- **1 drum machine**
- **1 sampler/MPC**
- **1 bass source**
- **1 main keys** (piano/Rhodes/workstation)
- **1 synth** (leads/pads)
- **1-2 accent instruments** (strings/brass/flute/sax/percussion)
- **0-1 texture** (vinyl crackle/Portastudio)

### Redundancy Rules (IMPORTANT)
❌ **DON'T include both:**
- "TR-808" AND "TR-808 kick/snare/hat" (same thing)
- "TR-808" AND "TR-808 sub-bass" in instruments section (pick one)

❌ **DON'T stack variants in same track:**
- Motif + Motif XF (pick one)
- Triton + Triton Extreme (pick one)
- Virus TI + TI2 (pick one)
- Prophet-5 + Prophet-6 (pick one)
- Juno-60 + Juno-106 (pick one)
- MPC Live + MPC X (pick one as "modern MPC")

## Writing Instrument Descriptions

**Formula:** `[Instrument Name] ([2-3 sonic characteristics])`

**Examples:**
- ❌ "808 bass" (too generic)
- ✅ "TR-808 sub-bass (thunderous, sliding, booming)"

- ❌ "piano" (too generic)
- ✅ "Fender Rhodes (warm soul chords, bell-like)"

- ❌ "synth" (too generic)
- ✅ "Moog Subsequent 37 (fat bass, aggressive, punchy)"

## Fullness & Density Descriptors

Use these in the "mix" section of prompts:

### Low End
- "tight low end" / "808 dominant" / "sub-bass prominent"
- "deep rumble" / "thunderous bass" / "booming low frequencies"

### Mid Range
- "vocals cutting through" / "horns punctuating"
- "warm mid presence" / "rich harmonics"

### High End
- "open highs" / "crisp hi-hats" / "bright presence"
- "metallic shimmer" / "airy top end"

### Stereo & Space
- "wide stereo spread" / "panoramic mix"
- "room tone" / "spatial depth"

### Overall Density
- "richly layered" / "dense mix" / "wall of sound"
- "full arrangement" / "complex textures"

### Dynamics
- "punchy" / "hard-hitting" / "impactful"
- "driving momentum" / "building intensity"

### Avoid (Too Vague)
- ❌ "good mix" / "nice sound" / "quality production"
- ❌ "professional" alone (pair with specifics)

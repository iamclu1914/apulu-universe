# Roc-A-Fella / Soul Era

## Just Blaze

**Era:** Early-mid 2000s Roc-A-Fella

**Signature Sound:**
- Massive soul sample chops (sped-up chipmunk soul)
- Layered orchestral arrangements (horns, strings)
- Hard-hitting drums with swing
- Triumphant, anthemic energy
- Big room reverb on drums

**Key Instruments:**
- Chopped soul vocal samples (high-pitched, sped-up)
- Trumpet sections (vintage soul brass stabs)
- Violin sections (sweeping cinematic builds)
- Roland TR-808 (punchy kicks)
- Live snare (crisp, layered)
- Rhodes electric piano (warm vintage chords)

**BPM Range:** 85-98

**Emotion Keywords:** Triumphant, anthemic, soulful, powerful, victorious

**Billboard Hits:** "Stand Up" (Ludacris), "Touch the Sky" (Kanye West), "Empire State of Mind" (Jay-Z)

**Style Prompt Fragment:**
```
Early 2000s anthemic soul-sampling hip-hop. Chopped soul vocal samples (sped-up chipmunk), layered trumpet sections (vintage brass stabs), sweeping violin builds, Roland TR-808 punchy kicks, crisp live snare, Rhodes warm chords, triumphant orchestral energy.
```

# Timbaland School

## Timbaland

**Era:** Late 90s-2000s

**Signature Sound:**
- ASR-10 sampler drums (distinctive digital crunch)
- Korg Triton synth sounds
- Stuttering/chopped drum patterns
- Beatbox elements and vocal percussion
- World music samples (Indian, Middle Eastern)
- Reversed audio effects
- Minimal approach (2-3 core sounds)
- Tape-saturated strings
- Marching band energy on big moments

**Key Instruments:**
- Ensoniq ASR-10 drums (digital crunch, distinctive character)
- Korg Triton synths (signature lead sounds)
- Beatbox vocal percussion (mouth drums, clicks)
- World percussion samples (tablas, dhol)
- Reversed vocal chops
- Tape-saturated strings (warm analog)
- Marching snare rolls (energetic builds)

**BPM Range:** 90-110

**Emotion Keywords:** Futuristic, bouncy, innovative, hypnotic, groove-heavy

**Billboard Hits:** "Big Pimpin'" (Jay-Z), "Try Again" (Aaliyah), "SexyBack" (Justin Timberlake), "Apologize" (OneRepublic)

**Style Prompt Fragment:**
```
Late 90s futuristic bounce hip-hop. ASR-10 drums (digital crunch), Korg Triton synths (cutting leads), stuttering chopped patterns, beatbox vocal percussion, world samples (tablas), reversed audio chops, minimal 2-3 core sounds, marching band energy on drops.
```

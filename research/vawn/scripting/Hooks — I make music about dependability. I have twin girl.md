# Hooks — I make music about dependability. I have twin girl

*quiet, heavy, intimate — pulls you in before you realize it*

> [!example] Visual Hook
> Close-up of two baby shoes side by side on a studio console, beat slowly fading in underneath

**Text Overlay:** ==THIS IS WHAT PRESSURE SOUNDS LIKE==

*calm defiance, anti-hype, grounded authority*

**Text Overlay:** ==NOT AN ESCAPE. A RECORD.==

**Text Overlay:** ==THAT TAKE MADE THE ALBUM==

## Project Overview

**Objective**: Integrate ByteDance's Seedance 2.0 multimodal AI video generation platform into Apulu Records' music video production workflow, providing Vawn with cutting-edge audio-visual synchronization capabilities for hip-hop content creation.

**Strategic Value**: 
- 80-90% reduction in music video production costs
- Perfect audio-visual synchronization for rap/hip-hop content  
- Scalable video content creation for multi-platform distribution

## Research Findings Summary

### 1. Access Strategy Research ✅
**Current Reality**: No official global API exists. US access requires workarounds.

**Viable Solutions Identified**:
- **TopView AI**: Professional subscription service offering unlimited Seedance 2.0 generations
- **Chatcut.io**: Community access via Discord invite codes
- **Cost**: ~$0.10 per minute generated content (via authorized platforms)

**Recommendation**: Pursue TopView AI business subscription for reliable, professional access

### 2. Implementation-Ready Prompt Templates ✅
**Deliverable**: Complete library of Vawn-specific prompt templates optimized for Seedance 2.0

**Template Categories Created**:
- Performance Core (rap delivery sequences)
- Urban Atmosphere (environment/mood building) 
- Narrative Movement (story progression shots)
- Social Vertical (TikTok/IG optimized 9:16)
- Lyric Focus (close-up emotional moments)
- Transition Bridges (scene connectors)

**Technical Specifications**: 6-step prompt formula implementation, 15-second segment optimization, platform-specific adaptations

### 3. Character Creation Strategy ✅
**Deliverable**: Comprehensive SoulCast 3.0 character development plan for Vawn

**Implementation Approach**: Hybrid methodology combining real photo training with archetype enhancement

**Key Components**:
- 25-30 photo professional photoshoot requirements
- Character sheet generation strategy (angles, expressions, outfits)
- Consistency maintenance across 15-second segments
- Integration with existing workflow via @VawnRef tagging system

### 4. Technical Integration Roadmap ✅
**Deliverable**: Complete Apulu Prompt Generator enhancement plan

**Integration Strategy**: Additive approach preserving existing Higgsfield/Kling workflows while adding Seedance 2.0 as first-class citizen

**Key Enhancements**:
- Audio upload and synchronization interface
- 6-step prompt formula builder
- Timeline prompting for multi-shot sequences
- Reference image management (@tag system)
- Multi-platform generation with intelligent routing

**Probability**: Medium (US restrictions exist)
**Mitigation**:

- Continuous monitoring of access options

**Risk**: Character consistency degradation affecting video quality
**Impact**: Medium (quality issues)
**Probability**: Low (well-tested technology)
**Mitigation**:

- Professional photography foundation
- Quality gates and approval processes
- Manual correction tools as backup

### Medium Risk — Cost Escalation  
**Risk**: Generation costs exceeding budget projections
**Impact**: Medium (budget overrun)
**Probability**: Medium (pay-per-use model)

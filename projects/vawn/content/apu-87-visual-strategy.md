# APU-87 Morning-Early Visual Content Strategy

## Core Visual Theme: "The Lamp Left On"
**Concept:** Domestic father imagery combined with lamp/doorway symbolism to represent dependability and presence

---

## Primary Visual Content (7:00 AM Deployment)

### Format 1: Emotion-First Context Reel
**Platform:** Instagram Reels, TikTok | **Duration:** 8 seconds setup + 22 seconds audio**

**Visual Sequence:**
1. **Opening shot (0-3s):** Close-up of lamp glowing warm in doorway frame, camera slowly pulls back
2. **Direct address (3-8s):** Vawn speaks to camera: "I wrote this verse for my daughters in case I'm not here to tell them myself."
3. **Music transition (8-11s):** Beat drops, camera shifts to lamp again
4. **Lyric delivery (11-30s):** "Came home to find you'd left the lamp on by the door" over atmospheric visual

**Technical Specs:**
- Vertical 9:16 format
- Warm lighting (lamp as key light source)  
- Phone-shot aesthetic (per daily brief: "raw delivery" performs better)
- Minimal editing: single cut between setup and music

**Platform Variations:**
- **Instagram:** Include subtle "YOU WERE THERE" text overlay at bottom
- **TikTok:** No text overlay, let audio carry the message
- **Threads:** Static frame from the lamp shot with lyric as caption

---

### Format 2: Domestic Father-Artist Reel  
**Platform:** TikTok, Instagram | **Duration:** 15-20 seconds**

**Visual Concept:** "Building an album while life happens"

**Shot Sequence:**
1. **Wide shot (0-5s):** Home studio/living room setup, twins visible in background (out of focus)
2. **Close-up (5-10s):** Vawn's hands adjusting headphones, notebook with lyrics visible
3. **Medium shot (10-15s):** Vawn in booth/corner recording setup, one twin walks past in background  
4. **Return to lamp (15-20s):** Same lamp from Format 1, representing completion/home

**Audio:** Low-level track playback with natural home sounds (subtle, authentic)

**Caption Strategy:**
- **Instagram:** "The work happens in the margins of real life. #DebutAlbum #YouWereThere"
- **TikTok:** "POV: making your debut album while your daughters teach you what dependability actually costs"

## Visual Production Notes

### Lighting Strategy
- **Key Light:** Actual household lamp (warm, 2700K-3000K)
- **Fill:** Natural window light or minimal phone flashlight bounce
- **Mood:** Intimate, unpolished, authentic domestic setting

### Location Requirements
- **Primary:** Home/living space with lamp and doorway
- **Secondary:** Home studio/music creation space  
- **Background Elements:** Subtle family life indicators (twins' toys barely visible, domestic setting)

### Equipment
- **Camera:** Phone (vertical orientation)
- **Audio:** Phone mic + low-level track playback for ambient sound
- **Editing:** Minimal cuts, natural transitions, warm color grade

### Performance Direction
- **Energy:** Quiet confidence, not performance
- **Eye Contact:** Direct to camera for emotional beats, natural for ambient shots  
- **Movement:** Minimal, purposeful (adjusting headphones, turning on lamp)

---

## Content Gap Exploitation Strategy

**Differentiators from Competition:**
1. **Domestic Authenticity:** No other artist in comp set (Baby Keem, 6LACK, Smino) shows real family integration
2. **Anti-Performance:** Raw phone footage vs. polished music videos  
3. **Symbolic Consistency:** Lamp imagery across all formats creates brand recognition
4. **Father Identity:** Positioned as strength, not limitation or backdrop

**Algorithm Optimization:**
- **TikTok:** 21-30 second optimal engagement window (per daily brief data)
- **Instagram:** Emotion-first storytelling + carousel saves for highest engagement
- **Threading:** Cross-platform consistency builds brand recognition

## Success Metrics & Validation

**Engagement Targets:**
- **TikTok:** >1000 views in first hour (baseline for morning posts)

- Comments reflecting "this is different" positioning
